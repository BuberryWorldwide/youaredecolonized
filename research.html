VERIFICATION CHECKLIST

BEFORE BELIEVING ANY CLAIM:

Source check: Who originally reported this? What's their funding source?
Multiple sources: Can you find the same information from 3+ independent sources?
Primary documents: Can you trace back to original documents, not just reporting about them?
Financial incentives: Who benefits if this story is believed? Who loses?
Timing: Why is this information coming out now? What else is happening?
Missing voices: Whose perspectives aren't included in this narrative?
