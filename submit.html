SUBMIT A ZINE IDEA

EXAMPLE: AIRBNB IS THE NEW SLUMLORD

THESIS:

How Airbnb reproduces slumlord extraction with app-based convenience. They remove housing from local communities, inflate rents through artificial scarcity, and extract wealth from neighborhoods while avoiding landlord responsibilities. Same exploitation, digital interface.

LOCAL CASE STUDIES:

Barcelona, Spain (2015-2020): Airbnb licenses increased 700% while long-term rentals decreased 40% in city center. Average rent increased €300/month. Local families displaced to suburbs. City council data shows 87% of new Airbnb listings were entire apartments removed from housing market. New Orleans, Louisiana (Post-Katrina): 65% of French Quarter properties now short-term rentals. Families who rebuilt after hurricane now can't afford to live in their own neighborhoods. Property taxes increased 200% while local wages stagnant.

VERIFICATION SOURCES:

- Barcelona City Council Housing Report 2020 (public PDF) - Inside Airbnb data (insideairbnb.com) - independent analysis - New Orleans Assessor's Office property records - Academic study: "Platform Urbanism" by Srnicek & Langley - Local news: Times-Picayune rental market analysis 2019-2023

REAL ALTERNATIVES:

- Vienna's social housing model (60% of residents) - Community land trusts in Burlington, VT - Berlin's rent cap legislation (before court challenges) - Cooperative housing in Copenhagen - Tenant unions with direct action tactics

PRACTICAL RESISTANCE:

- Support local ordinances limiting short-term rentals - Join tenant organizing against landlord platforms - Document illegal Airbnb conversions in your neighborhood - Support hotels with unionized workers over platforms - Build community land trusts to remove speculation

WHAT MAKES A STRONG SUBMISSION:

  • Specific numbers: Percentages, dollar amounts, dates, quantities
  • Named sources: Documents people can find and verify themselves
  • Local examples: What happened in specific cities/communities
  • Clear pattern: How modern system reproduces historical oppression
  • Real alternatives: Not just criticism, but examples that work
  • Actionable resistance: What people can actually do today

WHAT WE NEED FROM YOU:

  • Concrete examples with specific names, dates, dollar amounts
  • Verification sources readers can check themselves
  • Real alternatives that exist today and work
  • Practical resistance strategies people can actually do
  • Connection to historical patterns of oppression
  • Focus on systemic change, not just individual solutions

SHARE LOCAL INTELLIGENCE

EXAMPLE: CHARTER SCHOOL TAKEOVER

LOCATION:

Detroit, Michigan - Corktown neighborhood

WHAT YOU'RE SEEING:

Local elementary school (95% Black, 89% free lunch) suddenly got "failing" rating after budget cuts. Within 6 months, Brightstar Academy (charter chain) opened 2 blocks away with fancy marketing. Old school lost 40% enrollment, now threatened with closure. Same students, same poverty, but charter gets praised in Free Press while public school gets blamed.

DOCUMENT SOURCES:

- Detroit Public Schools budget documents 2019-2023 (DPS website) - Michigan Dept of Education school ratings database - Brightstar Academy board minutes (public records request) - Property records showing who owns charter building (Wayne County) - Detroit Free Press coverage comparison (archives)

LOCAL RESISTANCE:

Parents United for Detroit Schools organizing against closures. Monthly meetings at Corktown Recreation Center. Demanding budget transparency and fighting privatization. Contact: puds.detroit@protonmail.com
