    THEY MONITOR ACTIVISTS • PROTECT YOURSELF AND YOUR COMRADES
    DIGITAL SECURITY
    Your phones, computers, and social media are surveillance tools.
    BASIC PROTECTION:
    • Use Signal for sensitive conversations
    • VPN for all internet activity
    • Encrypted email (ProtonMail)
    • Separate devices for organizing
    • Assume all social media is monitored
    OPERATIONAL SECURITY
    How you organize affects everyone's safety.
    BASIC PRINCIPLES:
    • Need-to-know basis for sensitive info
    • No planning illegal activities over digital channels
    • Meet in secure locations
    • Watch for infiltrators and provocateurs
    • Keep detailed records secure
    LEGAL DEFENSE
    Know your rights. Have legal support ready.
    PREPARATION:
    • Know your rights during police encounters
    • Have legal observer training
    • Know bail fund contacts
    • Have lawyer contacts ready
    • Document police misconduct
    COMMUNITY DEFENSE
    Protect each other from state and fascist violence.
    COMMUNITY PROTECTION:
    • De-escalation training
    • First aid and medical training
    • Self-defense classes
    • Rapid response networks
    • Conflict transformation skills

    CHOOSE YOUR BATTLES

    Strategic thinking wins wars, not just battles

    Not every fight is worth fighting. Not every victory advances the cause. Not every action builds power.

    STRATEGIC QUESTIONS:

    • Does this build our capacity to fight bigger battles?
    • Does this connect our struggle to others?
    • Does this advance systemic change or just reform management?
    • Does this build independent power or dependence on their systems?
    • Does this educate and radicalize people?
    • Can we win, and what does winning look like?
