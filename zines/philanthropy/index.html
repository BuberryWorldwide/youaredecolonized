BREAK THE DEPENDENCY INJECTION

Your liberation has to fit their business model

WHAT GETS FUNDED:

Programs that don't threaten wealth concentration

  • Individual scholarships (not free public education for all)
  • Entrepreneurship training (not labor organizing)
  • Charitable healthcare (not universal systems)
  • Microfinance loans (not land redistribution)
  • "Green" technology (not ending fossil fuel extraction)

WHAT DOESN'T GET FUNDED:

Actual systemic change

  • Worker ownership of businesses
  • Democratic control of resources
  • Wealth redistribution
  • Land back to indigenous communities
  • Public ownership of utilities
Translation: You can have any solution you want, as long as it keeps them rich and you dependent.

THE CHARITY TAX SCAM

How "giving away" money makes them richer

BEZOS EXAMPLE - PUBLIC MATH:

2020 net worth: $113 billion
2020 "charitable giving": $10 billion (mostly to his own foundation)
2020 net worth increase: $70 billion
Actual result: $60 billion richer after "giving away" $10 billion

HOW THE SCAM WORKS:

  • Donate appreciated stock instead of cash
  • Avoid capital gains tax on stock sale
  • Claim full market value deduction
  • Foundation invests and grows wealth
  • Family controls foundation forever
  • Net result: Richer than before "giving"
WE SUBSIDIZE THEIR CONTROL THROUGH OUR TAX CODE
Their deductions mean the rest of us make up the difference in public revenue. The house always wins. Even when they're "giving it away."

EXAMPLES THAT WORK:

ROJAVA - NORTHERN SYRIA:
Despite war conditions, Kurdish communities have built democratic confederalism: worker cooperatives, women's liberation, ecological sustainability, direct democracy. No billionaire philanthropists required.

30+ years of autonomous governance, cooperative economics, and community-controlled education and healthcare. Built through organization, not charity.

COOPERATION HUMBOLDT - NORTHERN CALIFORNIA:
Local network supporting cooperative businesses, community land trusts, and alternative economics. Shows how communities can meet their own needs without dependence on external charity.

THESE WORK. THEY SPREAD. THEY THREATEN POWER. THAT'S WHY BILLIONAIRES DON'T FUND THEM.

ORGANIZING:

  • Connect with existing revolutionary organizations
